At AxoModel, we respect your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal data. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle and protect your personal data when you use our services.

We collect personal information you provide when you create an account, such as your name, email address, phone number, and password. We also collect usage data such as how often you click certain buttons, how many AIs you train, the average time of a training session, how long you spend on the site, your location and where you scroll on the site. Finally, we also collect technical details which include, your IP address, browser type and operating system details. Cookies are not used for tracking non-essential data but are used to track strictly necessary information for the functionality of the service.

We use the personal information we collect for the following purposes: To provide our services: We process your personal data as necessary to perform our contract with you and to fulfill our obligations under our Terms of Service. This includes data about your usage of our services and feedback you provide to us. You have the right to opt out of receiving promotional communications from us at any time by contacting us. Please note that you have the right to withdraw your consent at any time. The withdrawal of consent will not affect the lawfulness of processing based on consent before its withdrawal. Withdrawing your consent will lead to loss of access to our Service.

8. Your Rights

In accordance with the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR), you as a data subject have the following rights concerning your personal data: Right to Access: You have the right to request access to your personal data that we process. Right to Rectification: If the personal data we hold about you is inaccurate or incomplete, you have the right to have it corrected or completed. Right to Erasure (‘Right to be Forgotten’): Under certain circumstances, you have the right to request that we delete or remove your personal data from our systems. Right to Restriction of Processing: Under certain circumstances, you have the right to restrict the processing of your personal data. Right to Data Portability: You have the right to obtain a copy of your personal data in a structured, machine-readable format and have it transferred to another organization, where technically feasible. Right not to be subject to Automated Decision-Making or Profiling: You have the right not to be subject to a decision based solely on automated processing, including profiling, which produces legal effects concerning you or similarly significantly affects you. Right to Withdraw Consent: If we are processing your personal data based on your consent, you have the right to withdraw that consent at any time. Right to Lodge a Complaint: You have the right to lodge a complaint with a supervisory authority if you believe that our processing of your personal data violates your rights under GDPR. To exercise any of these rights, please contact us using the contact information provided in this policy. Please note that we may ask you to verify your identity before responding to such requests.
